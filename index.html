Stop Air Pollution Now!





HOW YOU CAN HELP!

  • Cleaning Smokestacks and Exhaust Pipes
  • Laws and Regulations
  • Use of Renewable or Green Energy
  • Individual Level Prevention Methods
  • Raising awareness through campaigns and advocacy


Negative impacts of air pollution:

  • Harms human health
  • Variety of environmental effects
  • Acid rain
  • Eutrophication
  • Haze
  • Effects on wildlife
  • Breathing problems
  • Reproductive failure
  • Disease
  • Ozone depletion
  • Crop and forest damage
  • Global climate change
